Hi there! It's Yifeng (Ethan). I am currently a EECS Ph.D. student at the University of California, Berkeley (UCB), advised by Prof. Pierluigi Nuzzo. My research focuses on the design and verification of cyber-physical systems (CPS), utilizing contract theory for system abstraction, compositional verification, and AI-based methodologies for system design.
